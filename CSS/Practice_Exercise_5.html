Main Responsibilities for an Artificial Intelligence Developer

The exact responsibilities of an AI developer will depend on an individual's specific position, but here is an overview of typical responsibilities, as listed by coding school Le Wagon:

  • Solve problems using AI software. AI developers utilize their expertise in algorithms and machine learning to optimize business processes, support data-driven decisions and promote efficiency.
  • Present AI systems in an approachable way. Effective communication is key; especially when leadership is not well-versed in the latest AI technologies. It is up to the AI developer to ensure widespread understanding as needed.
  • Design, implement and finetune AI systems. AI developers are responsible for designing and building AI systems from the ground up, ensuring they align with specific project goals.
  • Invest in ongoing education. Staying up to date on the latest AI technologies is crucial in a competitive landscape, with the latest best practices, tools and techniques all needed to move forward.
  • Be a leader. AI developers often take on a mentorship role, helping team members understand and apply AI technologies to ensure smooth adoption and use.
