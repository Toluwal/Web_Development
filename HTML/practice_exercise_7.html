Environmental Management

Published on June 17, 2025 by IERE Team

Environmental management is the process of strategically planning, organizing, and controlling activities that impact the environment to protect natural resources, minimize pollution, and promote sustainability. It encompasses a broad range of approaches, from regulatory compliance to proactive conservation initiatives, aimed at balancing human needs with the preservation of the environment for present and future generations.

Understanding Environmental Management

Environmental management is not simply about cleaning up pollution; it’s about preventing environmental problems from occurring in the first place. It’s a multi-faceted discipline that draws on knowledge from various fields, including ecology, chemistry, engineering, economics, and law.

Did You Know?

The goal is to find sustainable solutions that allow us to meet our needs without compromising the health of the planet. At its core, environmental management is about responsible stewardship.
